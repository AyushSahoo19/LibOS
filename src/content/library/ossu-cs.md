## Overview
The OSSU curriculum is a complete education in computer science using online materials. It's not just for career seekers or enthusiasts, but for those who want a proper, well-rounded grounding in concepts fundamental to all computing disciplines.

## Features
- **University Standard:** Curriculum modeled after the top CS programs in the US.
- **Top Quality Resources:** Uses MOOCs from Harvard, MIT, Princeton, and more.
- **Structured:** Organized into Intro, Core, and Advanced CS topics.

## Use Cases
- Gaining a formal-equivalent CS background for free.
- Systematically mastering the fundamentals of computing.
- Supplementing a bootcamp or self-taught web dev background.
