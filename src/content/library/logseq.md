## Overview
Logseq is a privacy-first, open-source knowledge management and collaboration platform. It focuses on privacy, longevity, and user-control.

## Features
- **Local-First:** Data stays as plain text files on your machine.
- **Bi-directional:** Interconnected graph of knowledge nodes.
- **Open Format:** Built on Markdown and Org-mode.

## Use Cases
- Personal "second brain" development.
- Project and task logging.
- Organizing research and academic notes.
